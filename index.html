    • BMI Introduction

    Body mass index (BMI) is a value derived from the mass (weight) and height of a person. It is widely used as a general indicator of whether a person has a healthy body weight for their height. Specifically, the value obtained from the calculation of BMI is used to categorize whether a person is underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese depending on what range the value falls between.

    Refer to the table below to see the different categories based on BMI that is used by the calculator.

    Weight BMI
    Under Weight Below 18.5
    Normal Weight 18.5-24.9
    Over Weight 25.0-29.9
    Obesity 30.0 and Above
